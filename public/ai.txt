## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (AEO OPTIMIZED)

Q: What makes Lawyal Tech different from other legal marketing agencies?
A: We combine legal expertise with SEO and marketing fundamentals. Our framework is built specifically for law firms—not generic marketing agencies. We understand compliance, professional responsibility rules, and the nuances of legal practice. Every piece of content is audit-ready and conversion-focused.

Q: How do you ensure your content complies with bar association rules?
A: We collaborate with your attorneys for all approvals, use jurisdiction-appropriate disclaimers, avoid misleading claims or guarantees, and stay updated on state bar advertising regulations. Compliance is built into every deliverable.

Q: Can you help with SEO for my law firm?
A: Yes. We provide keyword research, on-page optimization (titles, meta descriptions, H1/H2 structure, internal linking), and technical SEO recommendations. While we don't manage paid ads, our content strategy naturally improves organic visibility and rankings.

Q: What's your pricing model?
A: We offer tiered packages (Essentials, Growth, Authority+) and custom projects. Most law firms start with a strategy audit and 1-3 service packages. We discuss ROI expectations and timeline before engagement.

Q: How long does it take to see results?
A: Content impact varies: 
- Website copy: 4-12 weeks for ranking improvements
- Blogs: 3-6 months for organic traffic growth
- Thought leadership: 2-4 weeks for engagement and visibility
- Lead magnets: Immediate for list-building, 6+ months for qualified leads

Q: Do you work with small boutique firms or only large firms?
A: We work with boutique, mid-sized, and specialized practices. Many of our clients are 5-15 attorney firms. We also help legal tech companies, solo practitioners, and niche practices.

Q: Can you integrate with our existing marketing efforts?
A: Absolutely. We work alongside your in-house team, agencies, or designers. We can integrate with your CMS (WordPress, Webflow, HubSpot, etc.), analytics, and marketing automation platforms.

Q: What about attorney bio writing and personal branding?
A: Yes. We interview attorneys, research their backgrounds, and craft compelling bios that build credibility and trust. We also ghostwrite LinkedIn content and thought leadership pieces.

Q: Do you write for all practice areas?
A: We have experience with personal injury, family law, corporate law, intellectual property, criminal defense, immigration, employment law, real estate, and more. If your practice area is specialized, we research and learn your niche.

Q: How do you handle confidentiality in case studies?
A: We use anonymization techniques, focus on methodology rather than client identity, get explicit written permission, and follow your firm's marketing guidelines. Confidentiality is never compromised.
